Quick start

  • The 🧊 Add Body and ✏️ Sketch menus create shapes — primitives, extrudes and revolves.
  • The Feature Timeline (left) is your editable history — click any feature to change its numbers later.
  • Select two bodies (Ctrl/Cmd-click the timeline or Shift-click in 3D), then Combine, Cut or Intersect.
  • 🔍 Inspect shows mass properties — volume, surface area, centroid and estimated weight.
